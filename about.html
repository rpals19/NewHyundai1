About Lakshmi Hyundai

Indias no 1 Hyundai car dealer.

Creating Memorable
Customer Journeys

Since 1998

Lakshmi Hyundai expanded into the four-wheeler sector after successfully establishing a base of two-wheeler dealerships. Since its inception, Lakshmi Hyundai has been instrumental in increasing the retail sales of Hyundai cars in Andhra Pradesh.



Embarking on our founder’s vision for Lakshmi Hyundai, coupled with strategic management and operations, we expanded our footprint in places such as Visakhapatnam, Rajahmundry, Vijayanagaram, and Sangareddy in Andhra Pradesh.



Once the dealerships in Andhra Pradesh were established and fully functional, Lakshmi Hyundai forayed its services into Karnataka and Tamil Nadu with various outlets in Bangalore and Chennai.
