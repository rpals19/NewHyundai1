What is Automobile Insurance?

Car insurance protects you against financial losses if you’re involved in an accident or if the car is damaged. Insurance is a contract between you and your insurance company where you agree to pay a premium in exchange for protection against financial losses if you or your car meets with an accident.

Automobile Insurance covers:

  1. Vehicle Damages (To your car and other vehicles involved in the accident.)
  2. Property Damage
  3. Medical or Funeral Expenses
  4. Legal Expenses
